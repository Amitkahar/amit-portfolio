PROJECT / 01

Zomato Data Analysis

Restaurants lacked clarity on how pricing affected customer ratings — they were making pricing decisions blind. The dataset was raw and unstructured across multiple cities.

Higher pricing did not always correlate with higher ratings — customer preference varied significantly by city and cuisine type.
Excel SQL Power BI Tableau
Impact
Enables businesses to optimize pricing strategy and improve customer targeting by city — moving from guesswork to data-driven decisions.

Identified critical trends in patient data and operational metrics that were previously buried across separate spreadsheets.
Excel SQL Power BI Tableau

Improved decision-making visibility for healthcare operations — reducing the time from data to insight significantly.
